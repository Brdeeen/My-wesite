7 Deadly Sins

7 Deadly Sins, also known as 7ds, is an anime about 7 heroes that were framed for murder of a grandmaster ( Grandmaster Zaratros) and they become fugitives, until the Princess of liones goes on a search for the Seven Deadly SIns. She stumbles into Meliodas's bar, the boar hat in rusted armor and passes out. Meliodas, Hawk, and Elizabeth have to find the rest of the seven deadly sins to save lioness from the holy knights that have taken over.

Trinity Seven

Trinity Seven is about the Main male protagonist, Arata who finds his world turned upside-down one morning when a magical disaster known as a "Breakdown Phenomenon" destroys his hometown and claims the life of his childhood friend.

The Daily Life Of An Immortal King

The Daily Life Of An immortal King is about a boy,Wang Ling who is a near-invincible existence with powers far beyond his control. But now that he's sixteen, he faces his greatest battle yet - Senior High School. Wang Ling is a near-invincible existence with powers far beyond his control.

Black Cover

Black Clover is an anime that focuses on Asta, who a young orphan who is left to be raised in an orphanage alongside his fellow orphan, Yuno. While everyone is born with the ability to utilize mana in the form of magical power, Asta, with no magic however, instead focuses on physical strength.
